Simple Memory Game with Countdown Timer


The goal is to correctly input a secret sequence of letters and numbers after viewing it briefly.



Game Flow

  1. 1. Player selects a Difficulty (Easy, Medium, or Hard).
  2. 2. The sequence flashes on the screen for a specific Viewing Time.
  3. 3. The sequence disappears, and the player inputs what they remember.
  4. 4. If the input is correct, the player earns a reward.



Difficulty Settings

The difficulty determines the length of the sequence and the time the player has to memorize it. Sequences use random capital letters (A-Z) and numbers (0-9).


Easy Difficulty

Sequence Composition: 4 Letters + 2 Number (6 total elements)

Viewing Time: 6 seconds


Medium Difficulty

Sequence Composition: 5 Letters + 3 Numbers (8 total elements)

Viewing Time: 4 seconds


Hard Difficulty

Sequence Composition: 6 Letters + 4 Numbers (10 total elements)

Viewing Time: 3 seconds




Reward System

  • Reward: Correctly inputting the sequence unlocks a new picture.
  • Download: The player can freely download it as their reward.
